After the Fire

Release Date: Coming Soon Post-Apocalyptic RPG

After the Fire: The Last Brazilian is a post-apocalyptic RPG set 15 years after a global nuclear war devastated most of the world. You play as Lucas, a Brazilian survivor who awakens in a bunker with no memory and a mysterious clue about the Project Hope. Exploring the ruins of Brazil, you must survive harsh environments, face dangerous factions, and uncover the truth behind the war. Player choices shape alliances, the fate of survivors, and the future of civilization. In the end, your decisions will determine whether humanity rebuilds, collapses, or falls under a new tyranny.
